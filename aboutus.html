About Us

When Technicality meets Artistry

Kalaraag is an initiative taken up by the students of RAIT, who have come together as one for the sole purpose of creating and promoting Art. It is a team of engineering undergraduates who also happen to be musicians, actors, dancers, writers, painters, orators etc.rait Kalaraag participates in Youth Festival organised by Mumbai University for students' welfare and development.It is a huge platform to develop performing and fine art skills.

Over 350 colleges participate every year and we have manages to create a niche amongst the top competing colleges over the years.Having participated in and also organised several state level and national level events, Kalaraag thrives to fulfill it's purpose of standing together for art as a whole. Kalaraag family has developed its own culture and legacy that is appreciated and followed by the members over the years and we welcome each every artist in our Kalaraag family.
