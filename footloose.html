RAIT FOOTLOOSE DANCE CREW was formed in the year 2008-09. It is a crew where all the dance enthusiasts showcase their love and passion for dance; a platform where people from different backgrounds come together and perform as one. It's about learning from each other and growing together. It's a stepping stone to learning and improving in all aspects of dance. Footloose gives engineering students an opportunity to stand out and make passion into reality. Among 40000 engineers, Footloose members have a distinct identity. We, at Footloose, have been entrusted with the responsibility of representing our college which demands a long term investment of efforts, time and hardwork. Hardwork at Footloose is tangible and reeks of sweat and relispray. We as a team are dancing since 10 years in collaboration with Kalaraag our cultural backbone.
