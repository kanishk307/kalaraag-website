Our Events

RAIT Kalaraag organises three major Events every year namely Kalaraag Utsav,D Y Fest and Commercial Show. Apart from the cultural fests organised every year,RAIT Kalaraag is responsible for all the cultural activities taking place every year.

Our Participation

RAIT Kalaraag every year provides a great platform to the students of RAIT to participate in the YOUTH Festival organised by Mumbai University every year. It encourages students to participate and showcase their art in various cometitions as well.

Our Achievements

RAIT Kalaraag has been successful every year in winning accolades in Mumbai University's Youth Festival.RAIT Kalaraag bagged overall second runner-up trophy in the entire Mumbai Zone in the year 2017.It aims at achieving more such accolades every year as well.
